# Starter Asset Placeholder Pack for Artifex

This folder contains a set of simple SVG files intended as generic placeholders when starting a new game project with the Artifex tool. The assets have minimal detail so that they can be swapped out later with final artwork. Feel free to modify or replace them as needed.

## Contents

### Backgrounds

- **title_background.svg** – A generic title screen backdrop with sky, ground and mountain silhouette, leaving space for a game title.
- **map_background.svg** – A simple map backdrop with parchment colour, paths and nodes to represent progression without referring to a specific location.
- **first_playable_scene_base.svg** – A basic side‑on environment for the first playable area, including walls, floor and structures.
- **first_playable_scene_animated_background_tile.svg** – A repeating strip of hills/landscape intended for looping backgrounds.
- **first_playable_scene_foreground_overlay.svg** – Semi‑transparent decorative overlay for scene framing (branches/edges).
- **first_playable_scene_state_closed.svg** – Placeholder for a closed door, hatch, chest or cabinet.
- **first_playable_scene_state_open.svg** – Placeholder for the same feature in its open state.
- **first_playable_scene_state_transition_01.svg** – Midway transition between closed and open (phase 1).
- **first_playable_scene_state_transition_02.svg** – Midway transition between closed and open (phase 2).

### Characters

- **player_gameplay_sprite.svg** – A neutral character sprite with head, body, arms and legs.
- **player_portrait.svg** – A simple bust portrait for the player character.
- **introductory_npc_portrait.svg** – A similar portrait placeholder for a non‑player character.
- **basic_enemy_or_hazard_sprite.svg** – A stylised enemy or hazard sprite for early gameplay.

### Objects

- **first_clue_or_story_item.svg** – A note or letter indicating a clue or story item.
- **first_key_or_unlock_item.svg** – A key‑shaped object used for unlocking or progression.
- **first_collectible_item.svg** – A gem or coin representing a collectible pickup.
- **interactive_prop_closed.svg** – A searchable container or prop in its closed state.
- **interactive_prop_open.svg** – The same prop but open to indicate it has been used or searched.
- **map_location_marker_object.svg** – A generic marker object to place in the world to denote points of interest.

### Icons / UI

- **game_logo.svg** – Placeholder panel for the overall game logo or title.
- **button_start_game.svg** – A button labelled “Start” for beginning the game.
- **icon_interact.svg** – An interaction indicator (circle with arrow).
- **icon_inventory.svg** – A simple bag/box icon representing inventory.
- **icon_dialogue.svg** – A speech bubble for dialogue indicators.
- **icon_map_player_location.svg** – A marker showing the player’s current location on a map.
- **icon_map_destination.svg** – A marker for a selected destination on the map.
- **icon_map_locked_route.svg** – A symbol indicating a locked or blocked route on the map.
- **panel_dialogue_or_message.svg** – A generic panel for dialogue lines or messages.

Each SVG is deliberately rough and low‑detail. You can edit the colours or shapes to suit your prototype.

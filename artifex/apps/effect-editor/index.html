📖 Artifex Workspace Guide

Welcome to the premium interactive multi-layer particle composer.

  • Layering System: Click 'Insert' from the top menu to drop a new effect layer onto your canvas. Use the bottom panel to reorder or hide layers!
  • Preview Grid: Displays preview grid columns from 1 to 16, and row levels from A to I exactly like the mapping grid.
  • Viewport Zoom & Pan: Zoom easily from 50% to 300% using the slider in the top right. Click and hold the Middle Scroll wheel click to pan.
